about the app

Take a Look Around our App

StudPargne is a project under construction with the aim of encouraging the saving of their money and allowing their integration into the banking system. This project is mainly inspired by a program launched by Mercy Corps (accredited organization in Haiti). This program is called AVEK What is AVEK? AVEK is a program operating under the name of Association Vilajwa Epargne ak Kredi. We have observed this program in the commune and we have taken the time to understand its financial effects on the users of this program. Why was it inspired? The most familiar thing to young people as an integration in a financial system is the balance. The balance with the AVEK is somewhat familiar but the Avek is more structured. Both have the same starting point, the savings; but they do not take the same path to the arrival point. The only big difference is that everyone has their full amount until the end. Be aware that you can withdraw a certain amount of money during the cycle.
